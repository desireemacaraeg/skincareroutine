About

At first I thought the bumps on my forehead were caused my fungal acne (malassezia folliculitis) but after switching my routine with FA safe products I notice the bumps are still present. However, it may be Comedones and all I need to do is exfoliate more often. Speaking on the unevenness of my skin, I notice I have spots of hypo/hyperpigmentation and a bit of Rosacea on my cheeks.

Products

Foaming Cleanser

Hada Labo Gokujyun Hyaluronic Acid Foaming Cleanser gives you the feeling of clean skin without stripping it of all its nautral oils. I use this twice a day, morning and night, to give my skin a reset to all the products I'll put on after.

To Buy

Oil Cleanser

On the days I wear makeup, this, The Inkey List Oat Cleaning Balm, is what I use to melt it all away at the end of the day. This product is not fungal acne safe, however, when washed quickly and thoroughly the fungal acne shouldn't be triggered.

To Buy

Chemical Exfoliate

Paula's Choice 2% BHA Lotion gives my skin a new life after using it. Those small bumps on my forehead aren't as noticeable and irritable the day after, but it can cause a lot of irritation to other parts of your face if used too frequently.

To Buy

Adapalene

La Roche-Posay Adapalene Gel is a type of Rentinoid to help with acne and textures in the skin. I recently started using this again, it previously irritated my skin a little too much, and I can't say I see much of a difference so far but we'll see. I only use this on the day after using BHA lotion.

To Buy

Moisturizer

Recently went back to Vanicream Moisturizing Cream, because it makes my skin feel nice and plump while fixing my skin barrier. It takes a while for it to completely absorb into my skin, but once it does it's on there for good. A very thick consistency!

To Buy

Sunscreen

The Skin Aqua sunscreen in the Gold version is my holy grail product. I've repurchased this over four times, soon to be five. It leaves no white cast, but still gives you a slight glow to the face.
