About

At first I thought the bumps on my forehead were caused my fungal acne (malassezia folliculitis) but after switching my routine with FA safe products I notice the bumps are still present. However, it may be Comedones and all I need to do is exfoliate more often. Speaking on the unevenness of my skin, I notice I have spots of hypo/hyperpigmentation and a bit of Rosacea on my cheeks.

Chemical Exfoliate

Paula's Choice 2% BHA Loition

Paula's Choice 2% BHA Lotion gives my skin a new life after using it. Those small bumps on my forehead aren't as noticeable and irritable the day after, but it can cause a lot of irritation to other parts of your face if used too frequently. I have repurchased this product multiple times. I'd recommend buying the travel sized version first.

To Buy
Retinoid

La Roche-Posay Adapalene Gel

La Roche-Posay Adapalene Gel is a type of Retinoid to help with acne and textures in the skin. I recently started using this again, it previously irritated my skin a little too much, and I can't say I see much of a difference so far but we'll see. I only use this on the day after using BHA lotion. Only down side is the price, around $30.

To Buy
Moisturizer

Vanicream Moisturizing Cream

Recently went back to Vanicream Moisturizing Cream, because it makes my skin feel nice and plump while fixing my skin barrier. Majority of the moisturizers will have glycerin or aren't as hydrating, but not this product. It takes a while for it to completely absorb into my skin, but once it does it's on there for good. A very thick consistency!

To Buy
Sunscreen

Skin Aqua UV Super Moisture Gel Gold

The Skin Aqua sunscreen in the Gold version is my holy grail product. I've repurchased this over four times, soon to be five. It leaves no white cast, but still gives you a slight glow to the face.
